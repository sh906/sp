SMART CONTRACTS

SUPERDAO smart contract is the cornerstone of the community to achieve "voting resolution" and "code autonomy", and is the underlying code to protect the rights and interests of the community, running on the Ether and Coinan chains, supported by miners for operation.

Smart contracts are the only operational standard for all community activities. Including community building RRC issuance, community vault management, voting resolution, incubation projects, etc.

-CODE 1-

Registry -Module

That is, "smart API certified smart contract" for database storage, metadata storage outside the chain and other functional services, using decentralized storage chain such as IPFS or SIA and other underlying technologies to complete the service.

-CODE 2-

Job -Module

The "AI Managed Service Smart Contract" allows the AI function tokens to be paid for initiating AI service requests to be temporarily hosted on the blockchain in a multi-party chain until the delivery of the performance is completed.

-CODE 3-

Agent -Module

A smart contract interface designed specifically for interfacing to external AI services. By enabling the arbitration protocol in advance, end-users can anticipate the final task settlement point of the AI service they will interface to.

-CODE 4-

GAN -algorithm

Based on the massive amount of high-value data from users, with a large number of events that have happened in the market, GAN algorithm and a variant of Sequence GAN are used to perform AI analysis on various types of data.

-CODE 5-

Quantitative Analysis

Quantitative analysis is based on a huge amount of data, related to these data mainly comes from community members voting to submit volunteer statistics community building distribution and built-in crawler module.

-CODE 6-

TRB -Module

The core task is to maintain and call the core algorithms to ensure the stability of smart contract operation. In the process of frequent calls to the system, the TRB function is used to ensure the smooth operation of the system.
