What We Do

SUPERDAO community is a DAO concept community, there is no centralizer of power, no centralizer of interests, community members jointly own the community rights and interests and realized by smart contract. SUPERDAO smart contract replaces the role of KOL in traditional community, breaks up the power distribution among each community member, through the way of voting governance, the community members jointly decide the community development, to achieve "code autonomy". Therefore, the common interests of community members determine the interests of the community.
Various community incentive mechanisms are developed in the SUPERDAO smart contract to motivate community members to actively build the community through community tokens (RRCs).
All RRCs are set up as multi-signature accounts through Gnosis Safe and managed jointly by global community members.
